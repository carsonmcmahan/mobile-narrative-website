About My Website!

My Mobile Narrative Website is about the Los Angeles Dodgers and their stats for the last 50 years, not including the 2022 season. I have always loved baseball from the time I could walk. I grew up going to Dodgers games and they have become my favorite team. I wanted to do my Mobile Narrative Website on something that I enjoy and know quit a bit about. Baseball, and especially the Dodgers, is something that I am very passionate about. I would also say that I know more than the average viewer but not as much as those die-hard fans. One specific question I would like to answer is why I did not include the 2022 season. I did not include it because I wanted data from all ready completed seasons. When going through each website I had found data for this assignment, the 2022 was not complete like the other seasons. Therefore, I just decided it best to leave it out for my website.

My data was simple to collect. I found a website where it had stats on the Dodgers since they were founded as a team. While it was not the office MLB website, I felt that the data was accurate and could be used. I did however use the MLB website to find data on those players who had received awards in the past 50 years. Lastly, I wanted to do something a little more fun than just straight up data on the team. I decided I wanted to find some information regarding the Dodgers rivalry with the San Francisco Giants. While it is not a lot of data, I believe it gets the point across. Unfortunately, most of the data is in the Giants favor.
